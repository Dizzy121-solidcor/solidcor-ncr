⚙️ Settings

🧹 Normalise customer names
Scans all existing NCRs and groups similar customer names (e.g. "ian williams", "Ian William", "Ian Williams") into a single canonical version. Preview before applying.
📨 Scheduled customer reports
Auto-email each customer their NCR summary on the first Monday of every month. Requires delivery schedule for FTR%.
📈 Weekly exec report
Email address to receive the weekly executive NCR report every Monday at 7AM. Covers NCRs raised, SLA compliance, top issues and customer FTR%.
🎯 Daily RFT report
Email address to receive the daily Right First Time report each morning. Shows yesterday's deliveries, NCRs raised, and RFT% per customer. Requires a delivery schedule to be imported in the Deliveries view.
📦 Daily stock scrap report
Email address to receive the previous day's scrap/remake stock report automatically each morning. Lists all NCRs where stock was scrapped or needs remaking so stock levels can be adjusted.
🔔 NCR alert email
Email address to notify every time any NCR is raised (internal or external). Separate multiple addresses with commas.
🧩 Component picker
Email to notify when a component pick is required for an NCR.
⏱ SLA targets
External NCRs have two SLA stages. Internal NCRs use the red tag SLA.
